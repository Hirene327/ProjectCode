SUMMARY

Executive Summary:

Mitoyaki (みとやき) is a combination of meatballs (ミートボール Mītobōru) and Takoyaki (たこ焼き). We combined the fried meatballs with onions and onion springs sauced in Takoyaki style. It can also be combined with potatoes and fries in the balls. We can also add vegetables, mayonnaise, mustard, or ketchup as the customer prefers. Mitoyaki aims to serve unique and affordable snacks to every Filipino and to people around the world. Our company plans to succeed in every part of our business, including our product, ethics, and the disciplines that our employees should have. We aim to bring joy and create memorable moments for our lovely customers while they're enjoying our Mitoyaki meatball-takoyaki-style dishes. The business will succeed in the near future because it was created with teamwork. Every employee did their own work and focused on accomplishing it every single day. It is built by an organized and well-trained production team. The Mitoyaki meatball-takoyaki style dishes will be competing with the other all-day restaurants in the country. Our business aims to serve unique and delicious meatballs to every Filipino in the Takoyaki style.
