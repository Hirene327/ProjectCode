WHAT MAKES OUR PRODUCT SPECIAL?

Mitoyaki (みとやき) is a combination of meatballs ( ミートボール | Mītobōru) and Takoyaki (たこ焼き). Meatballs are ground meat rolled into a small ball along with other ingredients such as bread crumbs, eggs, butter, and seasoning. Takoyaki or "octopus balls'' is a ball-shaped Japanese snack typically filled with minced or diced octopus, tempura, and green onion served with sauce. We combined the fried meatballs with onion and onion springs sauced in Takoyaki style.

Mitoyaki makers assure the customers a good quality of product and tasty meatballs with a twist to make sure that the customer will enjoy the unique meal. The Mitoyaki meatball-takoyaki style dishes will be competing with the other all-day restaurants in the country. We aim to bring joy and create memorable moments to our lovely customers while they're enjoying our Mitoyaki meatball-takoyaki style dishes.

Our business aims to serve unique and delicious meatballs to every Filipino Takoyaki style. We are giving Mitoyaki loyalty cards to interested customers, every time their order will be recorded with the loyalty card and will serve them a starting ten percent discount for every five purchases. The benefit of the customer in our product is a tasty, affordable and customizable meal along with drinks and discount from their loyalty cards.
